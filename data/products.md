# Acme Corp Products

Acme Corp sells three product lines for home and small offices.

## Acme Beacon - smart desk lamp
A USB-C powered desk lamp with adjustable colour temperature (2700K to
6500K) and a touch-sensitive dimmer. Beacon connects to the Acme app for
schedules, scenes, and away-from-home control.

- Price: 79 USD
- In the box: lamp, USB-C cable, quick-start guide
- Warranty: 2 years

## Acme Pulse - portable speaker
Bluetooth 5.3 portable speaker with 18 hours of battery life and IP67
water resistance. Pairs with another Pulse for stereo. The Acme app
provides EQ presets and over-the-air firmware updates.

- Price: 129 USD
- In the box: speaker, USB-C cable, fabric carry strap, quick-start guide
- Warranty: 2 years

## Acme Tidy - cable organiser
A magnetic cable management tray that fits under any desk. Holds up to
six cables of mixed thickness and includes a removable cable comb.

- Price: 24 USD
- In the box: tray, mounting tape, cable comb
- Warranty: 1 year
