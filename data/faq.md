# Frequently Asked Questions

## Shipping

**Where do you ship?**
Acme ships to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, the EU, and
Australia. Shipping to other regions is handled on request.

**How long does delivery take?**
Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days within the US, 5 to 9
business days for international orders. Express options are available at
checkout.

**Is shipping free?**
Standard shipping is free on orders over 75 USD or for any bundle.

## Returns

**What is your return policy?**
Unopened items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full
refund. Opened items can be returned within 14 days as long as they are
in working condition; a 10% restocking fee applies. Final-sale items
are not returnable.

**How do I start a return?**
Email returns@acme-example.com with your order number. The team replies
within one business day with a prepaid label.

**When do I get my refund?**
Refunds are processed within 5 business days of the warehouse receiving
the return.

## Warranty

**Are your products under warranty?**
Yes - Beacon and Pulse carry a 2-year warranty, Tidy carries a 1-year
warranty. Warranty covers manufacturing defects, not accidental damage.

**What if my product breaks after the warranty?**
Acme offers a paid repair service for out-of-warranty issues. Contact
support for a quote.

## Account and orders

**Do I need an account to buy?**
No - guest checkout is available. Creating an account just makes order
tracking and reorders easier.

**Can I change or cancel an order after placing it?**
Yes, within 30 minutes of placing the order. After that the warehouse
has likely picked the items. Email support and we will do our best.

**Do you offer student or trade discounts?**
Yes - 15% off for verified students, 20% off for trade and design
professionals. Apply via the website discount form.
