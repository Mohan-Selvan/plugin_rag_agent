# Acme Support

## How to reach us

- Email: support@acme-example.com (replies within one business day)
- Live chat: Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm Pacific Time, on the website
- Phone: 1-800-ACME-HELP (US only), Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm Pacific

## Common requests

### Order status
Check the order page on acme-example.com or click the tracking link in
the shipping confirmation email. If the tracking has not updated for
more than 48 hours, email support with the order number.

### Replacing a damaged item
Email support@acme-example.com with the order number, a short
description, and a photo of the damage. Replacements ship same day for
warranty cases when stock is available.

### Pairing a Pulse speaker
Hold the power button for 5 seconds until the indicator pulses blue,
then select "Acme Pulse" from your device's Bluetooth menu. For stereo
pairing, hold the volume-up and power buttons together on both speakers
until the indicators pulse together.

### Resetting a Beacon lamp
Press and hold the touch sensor on the base for 10 seconds. The lamp
will dim, flash twice, and return to its factory settings.
